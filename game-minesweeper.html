Minesweeper

Clear the minefield — two modes, timed scoring.

Mines: 0 Time: 0s Score: 0
Left-click reveal  ·  Right-click flag  ·  P pause
Rules
Reveal all cells without detonating a mine. Numbers show how many mines are adjacent. Right-click to flag suspected mines. First click is always safe — flag bonus: +10 pts per correctly flagged mine (max +100).
Easy: 9×9 · 10 mines
Hard: 16×16 · 40 mines
