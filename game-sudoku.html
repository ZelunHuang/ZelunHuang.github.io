Sudoku

Fill the grid — three difficulty levels.

Time: 0s Easy
1-9 fill  ·  ← ↑ ↓ → navigate  ·  Backspace erase  ·  P pause
Rules
Fill the 9×9 grid so each row, column, and 3×3 box contains digits 1–9 exactly once. Numbers you enter appear in green; pre-filled clues are dark gray. Invalid entries turn red.
Easy: ~30 blanks · Medium: ~40 blanks · Hard: ~48 blanks
